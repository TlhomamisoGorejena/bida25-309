OUR SERVICES

Transport Services

FLEET TRANSPORT SOLUTION DESCRIPTION Our fleet transport services offers reliable,cost-effective logistics tailored to business needs. we specialize in per-kilometer pricing, transparent payment terms and comprehensive insurance coverage to ensure both efficiency and peace of mind. We offer:

  • Vans for group logistics and Trucks for cargo delivery.
  • Smart management that includes GPS tracking, fuel monitoring and driver performance analytics intergrated into a central dashboard.
  • Flexible Contracts which include short-term rentals for projects,long-term leases for ongoing operations and pay-per-use models for startups.
  • Support services where 24/7 roadside assistance, scheduled maintenance and replacement vehicles are always ready to minimize downtime.
PRICING MODEL Our pricing is tiered to accommodate different business sizes and needs.
  • Standard Rate- P70 per loaded kilometer.
  • Negotiable Terms- Discounts or adjustment rates may apply depending on contract length, volume of loads or special arrangements.
PRICING TERMS
  • Initial payment: 50% of invoice due upon loading.
  • Final payment: Balance payable upon offloading or within 14 days of delivery note issuance.
  • Transparency: Clear invoicing and documentation at every stage of the transport process.
SAFETY & Insurance Please note that:
  • Every transport load is automatically insured by the the company.
  • Clients are strongly adviced to insure their own cargo separately for additional protection.
  • Insurance coverage includes accidents, theft and unforseen events during transit.

Mechanical Maintenance

MECHANICAL & TECHNICAL SERVICES We provide specialized services across generators, earthmoving machinery, refurbishment and general maintenance, ensuring reliability, compliance and long-term performance for our clients. Service Scope:

  • Generators: installation, servicing and mechenical maintenance to gurantee uninterrupted power supply.
  • Earth-Moving Machinery: We provide preventive and corrective maintenance for excavators, bulldozers, graders and loaders.
  • Refurbishment: We provide restoration of worn or outdated equipment to extend operational life and improve efficiency.
  • General Maintenance: Routine mechanical checks, lubrication, and repairs across industrial and commercial equipment.
Pricing & Charges
  • We offer free assessments on site visits and quotation before work commences.
  • Our labour rate for mechanical maintenance and repair work is P800.00 per hour.
  • Discounts for long-term contracts or large-scale projects are highly prioritized.
Warranty & Insurance
  • Our workmanship comes with a 6 months warranty coverage on all completed work.
  • Clients benefit from both warranty and insurance protection ensuring accountability.
Payment Terms
  • 10% mobilization fee is required up front and remaining balance of the invoice on completion of work or within 30 days of commissioning.
  • Clear documentation and invoicing provided at every stage of the project.

Plant Hire

PLANT HIRE SERVICES We provide a wide range or heavy machinery for hire, ensuring clients have access to reliable equipments for construction, mining and infrastructure projects.
We have:

  • Trucks for haulage and logistics
  • Excavators for digging and site preparation
  • Loaders for material handling and transport
  • Compacting Rollers for road works and ground compaction
Rental Terms & Pricing
  • All machinery hire rates are determined by the nature of the contract and duration of hire.
  • Pricing is flexible and can be adjusted based on the Bill of Quantity(BOQ)and project scope.
  • A 10% deposit and mobilisation fee is required before machinery is moved to site.
  • For sites located 35km or more from our station, transport charges apply.
