About Us

Company Background

AutoLab Enterprise is a multidisciplinary service provider established in 2000 by its founder, Mr.Edgar Gorejena.
Since its inception, the company has grown into a trusted partner for businesses across Botswana and the wider region,
delivering reliable solutions in transport logistics, mechanical maintenance and plant hire services.
Built on a foundation of professionalism, safety and reliability, AutoLab Enterprise was created to bridge the gap between
business efficiency and technical expertise.
Over the years, it has positioned itself as a key player in supporting industries such as construction, mining and logistics.
AutoLab Enterprise's core operations are provision of Transport services, Machenical Maintenance and Plant Hire.
AutoLab Enterprise's strength lies in combining cost-effective solutions with accountability and compliance.
Which is why every service is backed by transparent invoicing and negotiable rates, insurance coverage and warranty protection,
professional workmanship and industry standard safety practices.
